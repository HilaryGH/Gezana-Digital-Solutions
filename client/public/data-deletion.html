Your Right to Data Deletion

Under data protection laws, including GDPR and other applicable regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. HomeHub Digital Solutions is committed to honoring your privacy rights and will process your deletion request in accordance with applicable laws.

What Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • Email address and contact details
  • Payment information (stored securely and processed through third-party providers)
  • Service preferences and booking history
  • Reviews and ratings you have submitted
  • Uploaded documents and files
  • Communication history
  • Loyalty points and rewards

How to Request Data Deletion

1

Log into Your Account

You must be logged into the account you wish to delete. This helps us verify your identity and ensure the request is legitimate.

2

Submit the Request Form

Fill out the form below with your account information and reason for deletion. All fields marked with * are required.

3

Verification Process

We will verify your identity by sending a confirmation email to your registered email address. You must confirm the deletion request.

4

Processing

Once verified, we will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when the process is complete.
